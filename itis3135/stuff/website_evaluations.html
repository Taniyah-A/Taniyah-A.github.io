Website Evaluations

Mubasic

MUBASIC is a platform dedicated to providing high-quality children's music, featuring a growing catalog sourced from talented creators worldwide. The site aims to be the primary resource for children's music, offering a variety of tracks suitable for young audiences.

  • Contrast: The site effectively uses contrast with vibrant colors and varying font sizes, making it visually appealing and easy to navigate.
  • Repetition: There is consistent use of design elements, like color schemes and button styles, creating a cohesive look throughout the site.
  • Alignment: Most elements are well-aligned, providing a structured layout that enhances readability and flow.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped logically, helping users to understand content relationships and navigate the site efficiently.

MUBASIC follows the CRAP principles well, creating to a positive user experience and well designed website.

LINGsCARS

LINGsCars is a car leasing website based in Newcastle upon Tyne, England. This site offers deals for both personal and business use, including options for those with poor credit. The site features a wide range of vehicles from various manufacturers, ensuring quick access to in-stock cars.

  • Contrast: Some text elements lack sufficient contrast against their backgrounds, making them hard to read.
  • Repetition: The inconsistent use of visual elements, such as buttons and icons, can confuse users.
  • Alignment: Certain elements appear misaligned or cluttered, detracting from the site's overall professionalism.
  • Proximity: Information is sometimes spaced too far apart, which can lead to confusion about related content.

LINGsCARS does not follow the CRAP principles well, resulting in a poorly designed website, hindering the user experience.
