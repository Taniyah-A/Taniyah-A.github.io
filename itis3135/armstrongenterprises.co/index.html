The story of Armstrong Enterprises is one of hard work, respect for employees, entrepreneurship and making bold decisions. Found in 2025, we are a logistics and supply chain solutions company focused on optimizing delivery systems for local farmers and food producers. Our goal is to strengthen local food systems and support sustainable agriculture.

  • Our company partners with small-scale farmers to boost their visibility and access to regional markets.
  • Recognized by sustainability and entrepreneurship organizations for its role in transforming local food economies.
  • Leverage smart tracking systems and data analytics to streamline the supply chain.
  • Uses electric vehicles and optimized routes to reduce carbon emissions in deliveries.
  • Operates a user-friendly online platform that connects producers directly with restaurants, stores, and consumers.
