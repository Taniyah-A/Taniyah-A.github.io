Client Project Overview

  • Project Name: Nit Picky Confections- Portfolio Website
  • Prject Overview:
    • Function & Purpose: This website will serve as a professional online portfolio and business hub for a local at-home baker. It will showcase their baking skills, highlight their specialties, and display examples of their previous creations. Importantly, it will provide a seamless way for potential customers to contact the baker and easily request custom orders.
    • Intended Users: Potential customers in the Raleigh, Cary, Durham area and returning clients. Also the website will provide an interface for the administrator (the baker) to receive and manage order inquiries.
    • Content Overview: There will be a home page, about page, gallery, menu, order inquiry page, and a contact page
